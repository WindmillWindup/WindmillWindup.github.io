Windmill Windup 2007

The Beauty of ultimate in Amsterdam

2006 RESULTS

In the Women's division, Finland's Espoo used experience and surprisingly few players to take the Windmill Windup 2006 with a solid victory over London's Iceni.

Ultimate Vibration, from Cergy in France overcame a speedy Friselis team to win this year's Open title. UV carried the momentum from their Olympic Stadium win on friday night to avenge last years finals loss and claim their first title on Dutch soil.

Spirit Award

For SPIRIT, the 2006 women's division spirit award could only be decided by a raging round of Mingle Mingle between the ladies from the De Westin (represented by male volunteers) and Primavera. In the final round Primavera mingled fastest breaking the tie between the teams and earning them this years title of the Women's Spirit Award.

La Fotta earned this years Windmill Windup Open Division Spirit Award.
