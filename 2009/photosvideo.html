Videos 2009 from BicycleMark

All around the tournament. Bicyclemark with co-host Hilco 'saint discus' give an insight in the world of Windmill Windup Ultimate. Enjoy...

Mad mad Promo: Can you feel it?

Europe's largest outdoor grass tournament, and a general carnival of ultimate Frisbee lunacy is coming around once again. It all takes place in Amsterdam, June 12th to the 14th. Go to windmillwindup.com for all the info and to get yourself on a team.


Windmill Windup 2009 - Mad Mad Promo from BicycleMark on Vimeo.





Chapter 1: The Welcome

The introduction to Windmill Windup 2009 in Amsterdam. Including game highlights, pub quiz, sideline banter, and two hosts attempting to be funny.


Windmill Windup 2009 - Chapter 1 from BicycleMark on Vimeo.





Chapter 2: This one is entitled: Strange Hobbies

The theme here was to learn something more about the many ultimate players that come from around the world to play at Windmill. So in order to get to know the better, why not get into their strange or not-so-strange hobbies?




Windmill Windup 2009 - Chapter 2 from BicycleMark on Vimeo.





Chapter 3: Dunks, Food, and Samba

Part 3 in the epic saga of Europe's largest outdoor grass ultimate tournament. No frisbee was actually played in this clip.

Chapter 4: Mingle Mangle

This chapter features Windmill Windup's most infamous after frisbee activity known as mingle mingle. It also includes extensive coverage of the Beer Race, all from windmillwindup2009.
