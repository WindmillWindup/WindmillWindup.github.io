Windmill Windup - Ultimate frisbee tournament Amsterdam 17-19 June 2011

About Windmill Windup

Windmill Windup is the biggest annual European ultimate frisbee tournament on grass. The carnival of ultimate frisbee lunacy in Amsterdam originated in 2005 and this year around 1200 players from all over Europe will participate. It's promising to be a full packed three days of hardcore ultimate and exiting amusement in relaxed Amsterdam-style atmosphere. Come by and join the fun.

Sponsors

We proudly present the Windmil Windup Sponsors who make this tournament possible:

  • Lookfly

    Lookfly is the official main sponsor of the Windmill Windup. This British company manufactures eco-friendly and ethically sourced performance and lifestyle gear. Lookfly is an established brand in the ultimate frisbee scene. Lookfly has a special offer for all teams attending the Windmill Windup here

  • Bavaria

    Bavaria is an independent, family-owned company, and the Swinkels family thinks in terms of generations, not financial quarters. Bavaria is committed to sustainably brewing the best beer while remaining a responsible environmental. learn more about our green policies on their green website.

  • Bugaboo

    Bugaboo International is a Dutch design company that develops and produces mobility products. Bugaboo is known for its innovative and breakthrough design of strollers. Bugaboo on Facebook

  • GreenGraffiti

    You hear a lot about new media. GreenGraffiti® is the new media. Creating messages by means of the most appropriate new sustainable methods, we use communication techniques that are high impact on the audience but low impact on the environment. Sustainable by its very nature, GreenGraffiti® is not simply environmental box-ticking. We offset 100% of the water we use, and our processes are completely carbon neutral. Learn more about GreenGraffiti® here

  • Europe's Famous Hostels

    Europe's Famous Hostels is an association 30 independent hostels across Europe, from Amsterdam to Vienna. Selected as the best hostels in their locations, all our hostels offer great value, atmosphere and a safe stay in each city. The ethos of Europe's Famous Hostels is to create a network that guarantees a great experience while travelling in Europe.

  • De Lokatie

    The foundation 'De Lokatie' recycles used furniture and appliances to give a second life to usable consumer goods. The re-use of materials has a significant impact on CO2 reduction, the authority on applied science research in Holland indicated that the efforts of 'De Lokatie' result in a CO2 reduction of 550 kg, which equals de heating of 165 households for the duration of one year.

  • Blue Fox

    Blue Fox organises adventurous trips to Scandinavia. We want to show you the best the Northern peninsula has to offer. You can opt for one of the proven round trips or choose for a tailor made trip that is assembled to meet your specific needs and wishes. Welcome to adventure!

  • Ecodrukkers

    Ecodrukkers is a dutch printing company with an straightforward philosophy: high quality printing using a environmentally sustainable strategy. No use of chemicals in pre-press phase and a carbon neutral production process. Ecodrukkers makes only use of bio degradable ink en FSC certified paper. And the the whole printing process is powered by 100% sustainable energy. Ecodrukkers stands for a revolutionary development in the world of graphic design and printing!

  • TTWWOO graphic design

    Since 2008 the Windmill Windup's website and logo's are designed bij TTWWOO graphic design. TTWWOO will create everything ranging from websites, house styles and books to logo's, illustrations and full campaigns. You can check all their products and styles on their website.

  • Stadsdeel Nieuw West

    Stadsdeel Nieuw West, local government and part of the main city of Amsterdam, provides the Windmill Windup with an entire sportpark to use for three days of full-on ultimate. One of the priorities for the local authorities is to promote the use of the park and sports in general. Since last year and for the years to come they have proved to be a generous host.
