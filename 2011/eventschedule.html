Windmill Windup - Ultimate frisbee tournament Amsterdam 17-19 June 2011

About Windmill Windup

Windmill Windup is the biggest annual European ultimate frisbee tournament on grass. The carnival of ultimate frisbee lunacy in Amsterdam originated in 2005 and this year around 1200 players from all over Europe will participate. It's promising to be a full packed three days of hardcore ultimate and exiting amusement in relaxed Amsterdam-style atmosphere. Come by and join the fun.

    Friday June 15

    After the games, feel free to kick back and relax at the fields. Share a few beers with your team and fellow ultimate players. No need to go hungry: we will sell FRESH handmade burrito's, on a first come first serve basis, and we will fire up the BBQ as well. Yummy ...

    Charity Huck competition, 19:00 to 21:00

    From 19:00 to 21:00 trials for the Charity uck competition will take place. If you have someone in your team that can pull like a champion, make sure they are involved! Check out the Open Arms Malawi Pull Contest, sponsored by Bugaboo International BV. Winners get the glory, Windmill gear AND raise money for a great cause.

    Douglas Firs, 21:15 to 22:00

    Douglas Firs is Gertjan Van Hellemont, a young singer-musician-producer from Belgium who plays Harmony guitars and americana-like songs.
    Beautiful music, actually.

    The Hangin' DJ, 22:00 to 01:00

    The Hangin' DJ (Roel Idema) plays the alternative and obscure but doesn't shy away from the contemporary indiepop/stonerrock. Be ready for a balanced mix of Wave, grunge, rock-'n-roll and tunes that will set your feet on fire.

    Let's kill the moonshine, 20:00 to 24:00

    This interactive and real shooting gallery is based on Marinetti's manifest Futurismo. Futurists reject to the idea of the vulnerable, everyday human chasing the dream of perfection. Instead they choose chaos as a starting point for art, Modern art? One 100 years later there is still an urge to clean up. We invite you to take a gun and Kill the Moonshine. Shoot your sentimental darlings, make way for the Future.

    Casper Adrian, 20:30 to 21:15

    Casper Adrian's music is disarming and straightforward. In his songs he questions himself and the world around him. While he's not afraid to ask the big questions, life's simpler themes are paid attention as well. His style is a typical mix of folk, blues and pop and he strives to find a perfect balance between entertainment and awareness.

    'Beeldbuis: artistic movies', 19.30 to 20:30

    The Beeldbuis Filmfestival has selected some of it's best and finest shorts, contributed by talented filmmakers from Holland and Belgium. Beeldbuis is an annual short filmfestival, where every filmgenre is represented. For more info on Beeldbuis and taking part as a filmmaker (doesn't matter where you are from) go to: www.beeldbuisfilmfestival.nl.

    Into town?

    Teams can also make their way by Bus (see directions section of the site) to the city center to taste some eclectic international cuisines. Restaurants in the center (specifically the Nieuwmarkt area) are listed on the Amsterdam page.

    Saturday June 16

    On Saturday, Mingle mingle and Party!

    Dinner and Mr. Steel, 18:30 to 19:30

    Willing Wheels will once again serve delicious three course diners from the yellow restaurant bus. During dinner we will have the infamous Mr. Steel from Amsterdam back at the Windmill. Many enjoyed his chill carribbean sounds during Windmill IV and we expect the same response this year! Check his music here.

    Mingle Mingle & Beer race, 20:30 to 21:30

    We kick the night off with the 7th edition of the massive Mingle Mingle, a huge gathering of people dancing and mingling and laughing with eachother. The game is simple, grab yourself a random partner, arrange yourselves into the called out positions. Prizes for the winning duo

    Next is a new and improved version of the Beer Race - grab a partner, look for a sign up sheet, and enjoy a great start to your Saturday party night.

    Party, 23:00 to 4:00

    Then, the PARTY! Fully warmed up after the beer race, the boys from The Benelux will kick the party into high gear. THE BENELUX is a 4 headed wolf band, a vintage electronic shock of tasteful noise, beats and resonating vocals that makes you want to kneel on the dancefloor and pray for more. For a sneak preview click here.

    Once you're in full swing, DJ Double D and Windmill's own DJ Team with DJ Snowy, DJ Phreak du Fonque will rip up the dance floor with some choice tunes that will keep your feet bouncing till the early morning.

    Sunday June 17

    After days of intense playing the suspense is rising. Which teams will make it to the final? Take a seat in one of the comfortable couches and enjoy the the best ultimate that the Windmill 2011 has to offer!

    Musical surprise, around breakfast

    Sunday morning, breakfast, rays of light bounce from the fierce elephant. The players prepare for their final battles, one more coffee and a banana, stuffed away in the side pocket of a backpack. Something is missing, but what?

    Accuracy competition, first time-out during final

    In the first time-out of the final you can show your throwing accuracy by hitting moving targets. Full hits will be awarded with nice prizes, full details will be disclosed during the tournament.

    Windmill Huck competition, halftime of open final

    Following the Friday preliminaries we will have 3 finalists in both the open and the women division making their appearance in the final. Every thrower gets to throw their share of discs and make a run for infinite glory!
    Check out the Huck competition, sponsored by Bugaboo International BV. Winners get the glory, some fancy Windmill gear AND has done his / her share to help us raise money for a great cause.
