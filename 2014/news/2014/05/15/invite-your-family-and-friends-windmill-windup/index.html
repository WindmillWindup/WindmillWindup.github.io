Invite your family and friends to Windmill Windup

Written by Aljaž Jazz Gaberšek on Thursday, 15 May 2014 .

Getting friends and family to enjoy a nice Sunday, sipping cocktails, beer, smoothies or what have you. Sharing the elephant love for flying plastic at Windmill Windup 2014. Buy 4, get 2 for free!

Windmill is a great way to show the world what high level Ultimate is all about. Invite your family and friends for a day at the Windmill where they can watch high level games and well… experience the Windmill!

The ticket sale starts today, click here. Buy 4, get 2 extra free! All proceeds go to the development of Ultimate.
