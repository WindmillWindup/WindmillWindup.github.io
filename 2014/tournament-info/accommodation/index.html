Accommodation

Once again we will be setting up camp in our onsite tent city. Camping marshals will guide you to the "normal" or "quiet" camping options upon your arrival. If you want to stay in a hostel or hotel, please see our suggestions below. If you need any assistance on booking we will try to help you out, but booking is  your responsibility. It is advised to book hotel and hostel rooms well in advance since Windmill Windup is in the middle of the busy tourist season.

Camping

The camping area will be open as of 17:00 on Thursday June 12th, but if you would like to arrive earlier on Thursday please note that it will be at your own risk because there will not be anyone supervising the camping area before Thursday evening.

Camping at the fields will be on the Thursday, Friday and Saturday nights of the tournament. If you want pre or post tournament camping, you can find more information here.
