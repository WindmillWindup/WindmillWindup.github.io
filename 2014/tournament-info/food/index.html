The basics

At Windmill, we want to make sure that you enjoy every aspect of the tournament. This most definitely includes food. Our intention is to provide you with a few high quality meals during the tournament to ensure you stay fuelled for the games and sated for the surrounding events. We will also have a variety of snacks and sandwiches available for purchase throughout the day and don't forget to check out the variety of food vendors providing additional deliciousness such as lattes, fresh fruit shakes and the much loved NUTS cocktails. Our focus this year will also be on performance foods so look for these options on the menus as well!

Breakfast/Lunch

We will be providing breakfast on Friday, Saturday and Sunday. We encourage you to make your lunch in the morning from the plentiful ingredients at breakfast. There is plenty of food of course but in case you want more don't forget, our food team prepares and sells fresh gourmet sandwiches, pasta salads, cold sport drinks, sodas, granola and chocolate goodies.

Thursday dinner

On Thursday, you will likely be hungry after a long drive or short flight, so we will be selling dinner options at the field and, of course, the bar will be open till late.

Friday DinnerÂ 

On Friday evening after the matches, we will be selling delicious meals on site from 18:00 till 21:30. Veggie options will be available.

Saturday Dinner

We will provide dinner on Saturday night at the fields before the party at no extra cost. You will receive your dinner tickets at registration.

Top Up!

Sometimes you are just super hungry. Like when you just sprinted your ass off for 4 full games, pulled in big hucks and made huge layout D's. At times like this you don't just want a meal. You want a MEAL. More meat, more sauce, more carbs cuz you earned it. Luckily we got you covered. This year we will offer a big eater top up option which means that for a few euros you can add an extra helping to your plate and make sure you are properly sated. Top UP!!!

Dietary Considerations?

We are going to try very hard to meet your dietary needs, so please contact us and we'll get our head food guy to discuss with you.

Other Meals

Throughout the tournament we will have various other meal options available to ensure that you stay sufficiently sated and happy. This will include BBQ options during the final and snack options in various locations.
