Thursday, 12 June 14

Registration will be open from 18:00 to 23:00, register to avoid lines on Friday morning. Afterwards, meet & greet all your ulti-friends, grab a beer and enjoy the tunes of our many DJ's.

Friday, 13 June 14

Charity Pull competition tryouts will take place from 19:00 to 22:00 so come out if you think you’ve got the stuff or know someone who does. The top seeds in the tryouts get to compete in the final pull competition on Sunday. It’s all for a great cause, so in the end everybody wins and gets to feel doubly awesome for it!

Saturday, 14 June 14

Herbie doesn't look a day over... 

This year will be especially awesome as we will be celebrating Herbie’s 10th birthday so plan on being up late!

Sunday, 15 June 14

The Final day is here! Which teams will have prevailed through the days of intense play and made a successful bid to play in the finals? Those who didn’t make it can still prepare themselves for an afternoon chock full of amazing Ultimate, great events and scrumptidiliumtous snacks and BBQ treats.
