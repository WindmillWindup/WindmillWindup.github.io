We proudly present the Sponsors who make it all possible:

Bavaria

Bavaria is an independent, family-owned company, and the Swinkels family thinks in terms of generations, not financial quarters. Bavaria is committed to sustainably brewing the best beer while remaining a responsible environmental leader. Learn more about our green policies on their green website.

FIVE Ultimate

One big difference is that 5 Ultimate doesn't simply "import from China". They manufacture their products themselves and oversee every step of the supply chain in person on the ground in China. From a small factory, owned and operated by a couple and their son and daughter and where the wages and working conditions of the 40 employees are well above standard, they ship their apparel slowly by boat (because it's greener than shipping by a fast plane).

Their Seattle office runs on 100% wind energy and 10% of the bottom line goes back into developing the sport. They've teamed up with Ultimate Peace to build a little piece every day. Windmill Windup will offset the shipping carbon footprint of the Windmill merchandise sold at the tournament.

Stadsdeel Nieuw West

Stadsdeel Nieuw West, local government and part of the main city of Amsterdam, provides the Windmill Windup with an entire sportpark to use for three days of full-on ultimate. One of the priorities for the local authorities is to promote the use of the park and sports in general. Since last year and for the years to come they have proved to be a generous host.

Here are our list of partners:

Leaguevine Ultimate stats

Leaguevine is an online community of Ultimate players who have a passion for statistics, real-time updates, and pushing the sport forward to the next level. 

Get Horizontal Online Ultimate Magazine

Get Horizontal is a blog about anything concerning Belgian & European Ultimate. Because laying out for a disc is the most intense way of showing dedication to this little piece of plastic that brings so many people together. When you go for that disc and dive, nothing else matters. 

Sportpark de Eendracht

Sportpark de Eendracht is a multi-functional facility that supports sports, events and most importantly Ultimate Frisbee.

De Lokatie

The foundation 'De Lokatie' recycles used furniture and appliances to give a second life to usable consumer goods. The re-use of materials has a significant impact on CO2 reduction, the authority on applied science research in Holland indicated that the efforts of 'De Lokatie' result in a CO2 reduction of 550 kg, which equals to the heating of 165 households for the duration of one year.

Discraft

Discraft is the world leader in disc sports, designing and manufacturing consistent, high-quality sportdiscs for Ultimate, Disc Golf, Freestyle and recreational play.

Ecodrukkers

Ecodrukkers is a dutch printing company with an straightforward philosophy: high quality printing using a environmentally sustainable strategy. No use of chemicals in the pre-press phase and a carbon neutral production process. Ecodrukkers makes only use of bio degradable ink and FSC certified paper and the the whole printing process is powered by 100% sustainable energy. Ecodrukkers stands for a revolutionary development in the world of graphic design and printing!

K9 Beveiliging

When you're asleep they keep watch and keep us all safe.

Swifterkamp

We appreciate very much the success of Swifterkamp for the effort of knitting the Windmill crew closer to each other. In a nice little cottage, living in a community without electricity and plenty of disks. The crew learned to work together as one!

Knowmads

We want to thank the Knowmads group for accomodating us in one of our many meetings of the Windmill Project. Here is a little bit about them: 

As a school we educate young creative entrepreneurial spirits. We believe it is our job to enable, facilitate, empower and inspire our students to become change makers. As a platform we connect our students, partners, staff, advisory board and a wide network of professional and volunteer experts/contributors to share talents, creative ideas and inspiration. We aim to initiate positive change.
