What is Windmill Windup

The Windmill Windup is a yearly Ultimate frisbee tournament held in Amsterdam, comprised of three divisions of play (open, women's and mixed). The three day event is loaded with Ultimate action and off the field antics, resulting in an unforgettable weekend of intense sport and constant fun.

Since year one, we have worked to improve the quality of our tournament, and the increased awareness and scale have allowed us to keep our costs down, while still earning money for our off the field initiatives, such as our push for increased growth in Dutch Ultimate by fostering a more involved student Ultimate scene, and our connections with charitable organizations.

All of these achievements are made possible by the community of players that show up every year, and the Amsterdam Ultimate community, who continue to provide the Windmill with the necessary energy and expertise. We strive to provide volunteers and organizers with a platform for creative thinking, and this has been an excellent means for us to learn as we grow.

For more information about the Windmill Windup, please feel free to contact us.
