Special team offer

From our buddies at 5 Ultimate:
We are so excited to be heading back to Amsterdam for Herbie's 10th Birthday! As usual, we'll be bringing along our sweet tournament merchandise and super fun vibes. In addition, we've got a team deal for all of YOU!

For teams attending Windmill Windup X, Five Ultimate will pay all delivery and duties fees for your team gear, an awesome opportunity to save hundreds on shipping and customs fees! In other words, the prices for custom team gear are exactly what you see on our website! Simply finalize your order by April 30th and we'll have your brand new kit ready for you in Amsterdam. As always, Five Ultimate offers free art assistance and we don't charge any setup fees.
Hit us up at team@fiveultimate.com to get your order started!

Competing at Worlds in Lecco? 

Five is offering special deals for teams competing in Lecco in 2014. In addition, Five Ultimate will still cover shipping and duties if you pick up your order at Windmill Windup. Not attending Windmill? No problem! We will arrange to ship your order to you from Amsterdam after the conclusion of Windmill Windup 2014.

Where has your Windmill gear been?

Windmill gear travels around. A lot. In this section we will display pictures of Windmill gear all over the world. So if you have an interesting photo of discs, shirts and hoodies with Herbie on it, do send it to us. 

Remember to always take your Windmill gear with you when you travel !
