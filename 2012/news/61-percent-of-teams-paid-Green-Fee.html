Windmill Windup - Ultimate frisbee tournament Amsterdam 15-17 June 2012

About Windmill Windup

Windmill Windup is the biggest annual European ultimate frisbee tournament on grass. The carnival of ultimate frisbee lunacy in Amsterdam originated in 2005 and this year around 1200 players from all over Europe will participate. It's promising to be a full packed three days of hardcore ultimate and exiting amusement in relaxed Amsterdam-style atmosphere. Come by and join the fun.

61 percent of teams paid Green Fee

Thursday, 25 Apr 2013 You can check which teams have paid the Green Fee on this page Herbie says: Thank You! Haven't paid the team fee yet? Just shoot us an email and transfer your € 50,- to the Windmill bank account. Until all the teams pay the Green Fee, the Windmill Windup will pay for those teams that don't participate in this program to offset 100 percent of the travelling of ALL the players. We do this by investing in green energy windmills in Turkey. Last year we had 60 percent of the teams participating as you can see in this video by Steve and Herbie from 2012:
