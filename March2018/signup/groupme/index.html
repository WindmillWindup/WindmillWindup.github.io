Like last year, we will be using GroupMe in order to inform teams about game and match scores, upcoming playing fields and times and general information. Please click on the "Join on GroupMe" link of your team in order to install GroupMe on your smartphone. Make sure before the tournament that at least one player of your team is able to receive notifications. Reasonably priced wireless internet will be provided on site to send and receive messages.
