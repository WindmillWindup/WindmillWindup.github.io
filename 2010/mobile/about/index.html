About Windmill WIndup Frisbee Tournament

...the biggest annual European ultimate frisbee tournament on grass.

The carnival of ultimate frisbee lunacy in Amsterdam originated in 2005 and this year around 1200 players from all over Europe will participate. It's promising to be a full packed three days of hardcore ultimate and exiting amusement in relaxed Amsterdam-style atmosphere. Come by and join the fun.

The Windmill Windup is a yearly ultimate frisbee tournament held in Amsterdam, comprised of three divisions of play (open, women's and mixed). The three day event is loaded with ultimate frisbee action and off the field antics, resulting in an unforgettable weekend of intense sport and constant fun.

The inaugural Windmill Windup, in 2005 was a great success, and marked the beginning of one of the world's fastest growing tournaments. We had 300 players in 2005, and in 2010, we will host 1200 of Europe's best ultimate players.
