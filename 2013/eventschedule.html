Windmill Windup - Ultimate frisbee tournament Amsterdam 14-16 June 2013

About Windmill Windup

Windmill Windup is the biggest annual European ultimate frisbee tournament on grass. The carnival of ultimate frisbee lunacy in Amsterdam originated in 2005 and this year around 1200 players from all over Europe will participate. It's promising to be a full packed three days of hardcore ultimate and exiting amusement in relaxed Amsterdam-style atmosphere. Come by and join the fun.

    Sunday June 16 2013

    After days of intense playing the suspense is rising. Which teams will make it to the final? Take a seat in one of the comfortable couches and enjoy the best ultimate that the Windmill 2013 has to offer!

    Get ready for breakfast

    Sunday morning, breakfast, rays of light bounce from the fierce elephant. The players prepare for their final battles, one more coffee and a banana, stuffed away in the side pocket of a backpack. Something is missing, but what?

    Accuracy competition, first time-out during final

    In the first time-out of the final you can show your throwing accuracy by hitting moving targets. Full hits will be awarded with nice prizes, full details will be disclosed during the tournament.

    Windmill Huck competition, halftime of open final

    Following the Friday preliminaries we will have 3 finalists in both the open and the women division making their appearance in the final. Every thrower gets to throw their share of discs and make a run for infinite glory!
    Check out the Huck competition, sponsored by Bugaboo International BV. Winners get the glory, some fancy Windmill gear AND has done his / her share to help us raise money for a great cause.
