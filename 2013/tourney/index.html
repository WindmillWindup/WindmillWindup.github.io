Windmill Windup - Ultimate frisbee tournament Amsterdam 14-16 June 2013

About Windmill Windup

Windmill Windup is the biggest annual European ultimate frisbee tournament on grass. The carnival of ultimate frisbee lunacy in Amsterdam originated in 2005 and this year around 1200 players from all over Europe will participate. It's promising to be a full packed three days of hardcore ultimate and exiting amusement in relaxed Amsterdam-style atmosphere. Come by and join the fun.

Tourney Info

Welcome to the 2013 edition of the Windmill Windup -- we hope you look forward to the third week in June the way we do! Read on to find out about next year's tournament weekend, 14-16 JUNE 2013.

Team Fee

  • Cost (if we will pay your travelling carbon footprint): €190
  • Deadline: Friday, 15 March

Accommodation

Accommodation is camping.

We will be setting up camp in our on-site tent city, we will have camping marshals to show you when you get there. If you want to stay in a hostel or hotel, we provide information below. If you need any assistance on booking we will try to help out, but booking is primarily your responsibility. It is advised to book well in advance since the tourist season starts at that time.

Camping

The camping area will be open as of 17:00 on Thursday the 13th of June, but if you would like to arrive earlier on Thursday, you are welcome to, though it will be at your own risk -- there will not be anyone supervising the camping area before Thursday evening.

Camping at the fields will be on the Thursday, Friday and Saturday nights of the tournament. If you want pre or post tournament camping here is information about camping around amsterdam.

Food

Breakfast/Lunch

We will be providing breakfast / lunch on Friday, Saturday and Sunday. We strive to provide you with quality and quantity of food. Yum yum. We encourage you to make your lunch in the morning from the plentiful ingredients at breakfast. Don't forget, our food team prepares and sells fresh gourmet sandwiches, pasta salads, cold sport drinks and sodas, granola and chocolate goodies.

Thursday dinner

On Thursday, you will likely be hungry after a long drive or short flight, so we will be selling Surinam roti rolls (both veggi and chicken) on site. And of course the bar will be open till late.

Friday Dinner

On Friday evening after the matches, we will be selling freshly made burrito and BBQ meals on site from 18:00 till 21:30. Veggi options available.

Saturday Dinner

We will also provide dinner on Saturday night at the fields before the party at no extra cost. Once again, the Willing Wheels kitchen bus will be there to fill your stomach. You will receive your dinner tickets at registration.

Other Meals

Get ready to watch the final, but not before you get your (veggi)burger at our BBQ next to the finals field. we’ll make sure it’s easy for you to grab a beer on the way to your seat!.
