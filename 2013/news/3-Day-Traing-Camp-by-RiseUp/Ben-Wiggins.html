Windmill Windup - Ultimate frisbee tournament Amsterdam 14-16 June 2013

About Windmill Windup

Windmill Windup is the biggest annual European ultimate frisbee tournament on grass. The carnival of ultimate frisbee lunacy in Amsterdam originated in 2005 and this year around 1200 players from all over Europe will participate. It's promising to be a full packed three days of hardcore ultimate and exiting amusement in relaxed Amsterdam-style atmosphere. Come by and join the fun.

3 Day Traing Camp by RiseUp/Ben Wiggins

Wednesday, 12 Dec 2012 RISE UP Ultimate is proud to announce RISE UP Amsterdam and our partnership with the Windmill Windup 2013! RISE UP Amsterdam is a 3-day camp/conference led by some of the best coaches in the world. We will also be shooting a RISE UP Season on site in Amsterdam. All will take place leading up to the 2013 Windup in June. A full registration site will be available in January. LIKE our Facebook page, and check out more info, updates, and a reveal of coaches.
