How to get to the venue?

Read instructions below on how to get to "sportpark de Eendracht" by car or public transport

By car

Coming from the south (A2/Breda-Utrecht)

1. Follow directions towards the A10 (Ring Amsterdam). Turn onto the A10 going south-west (s108 - s101/Den Haag). Don't go faster than 80 if indicated, we have 'Trajectcontrole' and it might cost you as it measures the average speed!

2. Take exit s103/s104, towards Haarlem. At the end of the exit, at the traffic light, turn left onto the N200 (Haarlemmerweg, direction Haarlem/Halfweg). Follow the signs Haarlem and turn left at the third traffic light (direction s104, Slotermeer/Geuzenveld). On the roundabout, take the first turn to your right (direction Geuzenveld, Antony Modderstraat). Follow this road for a long time (it changes names) until you see the LIDL supermarket in front of you. Directly after the sharp turn to the left, turn right into Cornelis Outshoornstraat. Follow this road and turn left at the end onto the Bok de Korverweg. At the end of this road, you will enter the parking area. At your right hand you will find the Rugby Stadium where the main tent is located.

Coming from the south-west (A4/Schiphol)

Turn on the A10 (Ring Amsterdam) direction west (Zaanstad/Leeuwarden). Then take it from step 2 as displayed above.

From the East (A1/Amersfoort)

Turn onto the A10 (Ring Amsterdam) going north (Zaanstad). Take exit s102/ Westpoort. At the end of the exit, turn right onto the Basisweg at the traffic light, towards s102/IJmuiden. At the fourth traffic light, turn left at the sign for the s104 (on your right at the crossing there is a building with very huge the word 'Scherpenzeel' on it). You are now on the Seineweg. Go straight and pass by two traffic lights until you hit a roundabout (towards Geuzenveld/Slotermeer). At the roundabout, take the first turn to your right (Antony Modderstraat, towards Geuzenveld). Follow this road for a long time (it changes names) until you see the LIDL supermarket in front of you. Directly after the sharp turn to the left, turn right onto Cornelis Outshoornstraat. Follow this road and turn left at the end onto Bok de Korverweg. At the end of this road, you will enter the parking area. At your right hand you will find the Rugby Stadium where the main tent is located.

By public transport

From Central Station

Take Citybus 21 (GVB) with the destination “Geuzenveld”. The bus leaves approximately every 10 minutes until midnight and the trip takes about 30 minutes. The last bus leaves at 3am . Get off at bus stop 'J.M. Ulystraat.'. Follow the arrows from the bus stop to the front entrance of the sportspark.

From Schiphol Airport

Take Citybus 69 (GVB) with the destination “Station Sloterdijk”. The bus leaves approximately every 20 minutes until midnight and the trip takes 25 minutes on the bus and 15 minutes to walk. The last bus leaves at 3am.  Get off at bus stop ‘Troelstralaan’. Walk 500 meters or take bus 21 and exit after 4 stops at bus stop ‘Freule Wittewaalpad’. 

SUGGESTION FOR TEAMS IN A HURRY TO GET TO THE FIELDS : Take a taxi which will take 15-20 minutes, and should cost around 35 Euros for 4 people or less.  Or take a taxi-van for 8 people. If you want to organize a taxi in advance, please call +31206777777 (taxi company amsterdam).

Some tips for taking buses in the Netherlands:

  • generally the bus driver will be able to speak some English
  • tell the driver you are going to Sportpark De Eendracht and that you need to get off at Wittewaalpad: 'with the waal path'. Or bring a note with you ;-). Most drivers will be so kind as to tell you where to get off.
  • Cheapest way to travel with a bus or tram is with an 'OV chipcard'. OV chipcards are sold at all rail stations. Only one person can travel with a card. Check in when entering a bus or tram and don't forget to check out.
    The easiest way to travel is to buy a single travel card in a bus or tram, this is a bit more expensive. Around 3 euro from Central Station to the fields.
  • For other public transit directions, go to 9292.nl.
